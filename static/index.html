The living source of truth for Volt · OLED Lime (v3.0) — Mission Control's visual system. Palette, type, motion and components, rendered live from the active theme.

Identity

Volt. OLED Lime

A pure-black OLED canvas charged by a single electric-lime accent, with emerald, cyan, violet, amber and coral as the working companion hues — each with a job. Flat surfaces, hairline borders and texture fills replace glass; depth comes from shadow, motion and exactly one lime focal per view.

You-are-here lime (--accent) marks the active nav item, today, and the live element
